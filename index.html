Welcome to CityLiving.Pokhara

City Living Pokhara is a specialist provider of quality contemporary short term serviced apartments located in historic buildings within Central Pokhara. Whether you are visiting for business or pleasure, our luxury apartments offer the perfect alternative to a hotel for a short or extended stay. With comprehensive free wifi in all apartments and a regular housekeeping service, you will have the comfort of home without the hassle. Our apartments are perfectly located for both the business hub of the City and the classic tourist sights of the West End, Covent Garden, Bloomsbury and Westminster.

Amazing Locations

Incredibly central, the apartments are located in Clerkenwell and Bank, London’s design capital and fashionable loft district and financial hub respectively. The apartments in Clerkenwell and set within former warehouses within easy walking distance of Farringdon station. The apartments in Bishopsgate are set within an elaborate listed building equidistant to both Liverpool Street and Bank stations.

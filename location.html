City Living

Mulberry House and the Heinze Building are located just around the corner from each other in the heart of Clerkenwell.  This area of central London has a fascinating history and is home to London’s design industry, which hosts the annual Clerkenwell Design Week. The apartments in Bishopsgate are set within an elaborate listed building equidistant to both Liverpool Street and Bank stations.

These luxury apartments are well located within central London for both business and leisure.  The City, London’s financial business hub is within walking distance; St Paul’s Cathedral and The Tate Modern are close by; and the rest of London including Soho, Westminster, Covent Garden and The West End are easily reached by taxi or public transport.
